Chicken Wrap

Description

Creamy. Crunchy. Spicy. Healthy. These Chicken Wraps, complete with a homemade Thai peanut sauce, are a burst of color, rich flavors, and texture that can be ready to devour in just 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Peanut Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sriacha and Honey
  • Ginger and Lime Juice
  • Broccoli Slaw
  • Edamame
  • Carrots
  • Peanuts
  • Green Onions and Cilantro
  • Whole Wheat Tortillas

Steps

  1. Stir together the sauce ingredients.
  2. Cook the chicken.
  3. Toss the chicken in 1/4 cup of the peanut sauce, reserving the remaining sauce for serving.
  4. Saute veggies until crisp-tenderâ€”this will just take a minute or two.
  5. To assemble wraps, spread a spoonful of the reserved Thai peanut sauce on each tortilla.
  6. Top with the chicken, veggies, peanuts, and garnishes. Wrap tightly and ENJOY!
